How It Works

This tool analyses a research paper (PDF) and its accompanying code implementation (ZIP archive) to identify discrepancies that might affect the reproducibility or validity of the research.

  1. Upload your research paper (PDF format).
  2. Upload the corresponding code implementation as a .Zip archive.
  3. Click "Run Comparison" to initiate the analysis.

The system will then compare the paper's core claims and key methological details against the algorithms and architectures of the code, highlighting any issues that may hinder out-of-the-box reproducibility
